Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions or search our knowledge base.
How do I submit a support ticket?
Go to the 'Submit a Ticket' page, fill out the form, and click submit. Our team will respond as soon as possible.
How can I check the status of my enquiry?
You will receive an email update, or you can contact us directly via the Contact page with your ticket ID.
Can I attach files to my support request?
Yes, you can upload files (screenshots, documents) when submitting a ticket.
What is the response time for support?
We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours, Monday to Friday.
How do I contact support directly?
Use the Contact page to send us a message or start a live chat during business hours.
